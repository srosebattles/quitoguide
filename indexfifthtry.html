Welcome to Ecuador! We hope you enjoy your stay in our beautiful country. It is a warm and welcoming place.

Quito has many beautiful natural sights and fascinating places of historical and cultural significance. It also offers a vibrant nightlife scene with a variety of bars and clubs.

My tours draw on my years of experience living in the city and help you get the most out of your brief stay here. Climb the sleeping giant Pichincha, dine in the historic Centro district, take in the art of Oswaldo Guayasamin, view the breathtaking Church of the Society of Jesus, or party the night away in Plaza Foch.

My name is Sarah Rose- I'm an American expat who has been living in Quito for the past five years. I came to study abroad for my final semester of college, and decided to stay. I majored in Spanish language and my years living here mean I am fluent in Ecuadorian Spanish (and speak a little Kichwa, too!). I'm excited to share the things that make Quito amazing with others.
